Our Approach Has Helped Many Supermarkets

We offer support every step of the way to get your system where it needs to be.

System Consulting

We can identify the best combination of POS software and hardware to fit your unique needs. We partner with leading hardware and software vendors to provide our customers with the best available back office and front end POS solutions.

Installation Services

Our specialists will travel to your location(s) and install your POS system. We will set up and test all equipment and perform quality assurance to ensure everything is running as it should. Our team will also train your staff on how your new POS system works.

Maintenance and Support

Our Help Desk & regional field service technicians are here to help you stay online 24/7. If you experience an issue with your LOC POS software or hardware, we will take action to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. We also offer maintenance plans that include the latest updates to your system.

Custom Retail Software

We fully support LOC store management suite (SMS) software solutions designed to provide our customers with industry-leading features that define modern retailing. LOC SMS Software can be customized to meet your needs and includes the following features:

Point of Sale

Process transactions quickly through an HTML5 based touch screen interface

Back Office Management

Full-featured item maintenance, including movement analysis & profitability by category by store

Loyalty

Maintain customer profiles, build full loyalty programs, and deliver electronic coupons during or after the transaction

Inventory Control

Controls for receiving and cost of goods management, with inter-store transfers

Fuel Integration

Fully integrated fuel module, including pump management and cross-marketing

Warehouse Management

Efficiently manage ordering and shipping processes throughout the entire chain

Kiosk & Price Checker

Verify price, customer points, balance, and promotions in every store

In-House Gift Cards

Manage gift cards and voucher balances, including online redemption

Centralized Reports & Price Updates

Deploy tighter control measures with less interaction

Labor Management

Integrate labor costs into financial reporting and pull it any time

Security

Fully PCI compliant, certified with both PCI DSS and Visa PABP

Merchandising

Unparalleled sale and promotional options, offering virtually unlimited combinations and offers
